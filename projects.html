ROCK PAPER SCISSORS LIZARD SPOCK (RPSLS)

This game comes from the common children's hand gesture game rock, paper, scissors where each can win and each can lose based on order and type. Having five options increases the complexity, but it is easily solved by using a random calculator against an array for each player and then deciding victory over a series of five gestures.
